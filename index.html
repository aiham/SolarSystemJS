Representation of sizes and distances of planets in our solar system. A demonstration of the Gyudon.js Javascript library.

Github Repository

Notes

  • Planet sizes are to scale.
  • Planet distances from the sun (top left) are to scale.
  • However, planet sizes are not to scale relative to the distances (There would be too much empty space otherwise).
  • Moon sizes, distances and counts are not to scale or accurate.
  • Size of the sun is not to scale (It is almost 10 times larger than Jupiter and would not fit).
